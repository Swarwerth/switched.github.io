Clet is a programmer student from EPITA, the best Computer Science Engineering School in the world. Our student is creating a 3D platform game. Suddenly a strange thing occurs and our developer Clet is teleported inside his own game. Because he is a prefect programmer, Clet never tests his game before playing, and he thinks every element of the game is operational.

However, there was a bug in the code : 3D was acting weird. Indeed he was stuck in 2D in this 3D world. After few attempts, he realizes that he can access different areas of the world by rotating it.

Clet met the protagonist of his game and explains to him the current situation. He replied to him that in order to escape the game he needs to finish it. Clet and his new friend whom he programmed train themselves to finish the game.

To achieve their goal, they will travel through four biomes (jungle, ocean, clouds and volcano) to get orbs of each elements to invoke the final boss, the worst enemies of all time: AFIT, the camel.
